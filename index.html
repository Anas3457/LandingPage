F1 in schools

F1 in Schools is the world's most prestigious STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math) competition. This competition instills in kids lifelong skills as well as individual traits and attributes that will help them define their future occupations and objectives. The aim for participating teams is to design, build, and race a 1/20th scale car against other schools. Teams must concentrate not only on the car but also on their branding and identity, portfolios of their work, and a team pit display.
